Configure your mail server to work with GoodCrypto

  1. Configure your existing mail server. Postfix, sendmail, etc.
  2. Configure your SMTP/IMAP/POP servers to default to SSL.
  3. Configure your users to connect via SSL for mail.
  4. Configure your SMTP/IMAP/POP servers to require your users to connect via SSL.
  5. Encourage others to use GoodCrypto so your mail with them is encrypted.
  6. Whenever GoodCrypto receives a new key from another GoodCrypto Server, the key is automatically imported. GoodCrypto sends mail to the user reminding them to verify the fingerprint.

    If some of your contacts use PGP without GoodCrypto, log into your GoodCrypto Server and import their public keys.

Back up to the previous page to finish the GoodCrypto Server configuration.
